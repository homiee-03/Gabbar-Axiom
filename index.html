About

I turned my exam journey into a system — not a routine. My method is based on cognitive science and practical classroom results. I help aspirants build memory-first study plans using active recall, spaced repetition and evidence-based focus techniques rather than rote long-hour schedules.

My Scientific Approach

Active Recall

Learn by retrieval — flashcards, practice tests, closed-book self-quizzes. This builds durable memory traces.

Spaced Repetition

Spread reviews over increasing intervals — we design revision maps that make forgetting work in your favour.

Focus Science

Short, focused sessions with dopamine-safe rewards — Pomodoro variants tuned for long-term retention.
